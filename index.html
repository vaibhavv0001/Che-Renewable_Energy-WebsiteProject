About

Our website provides information about renewable energy, including solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal energy. We aim to educate people about the benefits of renewable energy and encourage them to adopt sustainable practices.

Renewable energy is energy derived from natural sources that are replenished at a higher rate than they are consumed. unlight and wind, for example, are such sources that are constantly being replenished. Renewable energy sources are plentiful and all around us.
